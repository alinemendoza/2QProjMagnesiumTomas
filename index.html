woMan.

A wonderful man!

Greetings! Welcome to woMAN, a website dedicated to women. The purpose of this website isn't to degrade nor ignore the value men have, but to stress the fact that women have significance and character too. Do you know someone who feels ashamed of being a woman? Does she feel ignored and not appreciated enough? Does she think she isn't loved? Well, well, never fear! This website will erase her doubts, comfort her soul and make her hold her chin up high because that is what she should do! It features evidence such as quotes, videos, a collage, and a short bonus essay explaining why we are indeed remarkable men.
